The annotated section sits in the final third of Part 1, immediately before the closing check-in. It follows the two most physically demanding chapters — 011 (hard face / soft face) and the kind eyes sequence (008) — and precedes the Part 1 close. The listening diptych (013–016) is the culminating practice arc of Part 1, placing it here is structural: participants have been prepared through pain recall, observation training, and embodied face work before they meet the fake-listening trap. Part 2 then moves into kinesthetic empathy and vocal prosody — the body layer — before culminating in the therapeutic touch sequence and the full clinical roleplay finale.
